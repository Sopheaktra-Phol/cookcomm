Chicken Noodles

A delightful and hearty dish that's perfect for lunch or dinner. This recipe features tender chicken combined with savory noodles, creating a comforting meal that's easy to prepare.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 30 minutes
  • Preparation: 10 minutes
  • Cooking: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 200g chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • 200g egg noodles
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 100g broccoli florets
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro or green onions, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the noodles: Cook the egg noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Cook the chicken: In a large pan or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken slices and cook until they are no longer pink, about 5-7 minutes. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  3. Saut√© the vegetables: In the same pan, add the garlic and onion, cooking until fragrant and the onion is translucent. Add the carrot, bell pepper, and broccoli, and cook for another 5 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. Combine and season: Return the chicken to the pan, add the cooked noodles, soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil. Toss everything together until well combined and heated through. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve: Transfer the chicken noodles to serving plates and garnish with fresh cilantro or green onions.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
